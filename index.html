Web Made Movies!

Welcome to our development hub! This is the evolving home for Web Made Movies, Mozilla's Open Video Lab. Web Made Movies is working closely with the Center for Development of Open Technology at Seneca College and contributors like you to develop open video technologies, including Popcorn.js.

What is Popcorn.js?

Popcorn is a javascript library for manipulating open video on the web, with a focus on integrating semantic content with HTML5 video. We're actively working on other features such as manipulating media fragments, integrating with SVG+Canvas, CSS transitions and other standards-based approaches to video on the web.
